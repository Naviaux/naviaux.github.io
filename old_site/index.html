Programs

2015-01-18

I've decided to start keeping a changelog and download link to the programs I'm developing on this webpage, both for organization and consistency sake.

There are currently two programs that I've added to the website: BorderlandsBot and Osu Music Player

At the moment BorderlandsBot is under heavy development, but when it is done people who speedrun any Borderlands game on Twitch will be able to add a bot to their channel (aka BorderlandsBot) and it will have general commands for the Borderlands games for questions that are asked fairly often. Once it is done, I plan for it to have custom commands, timers, and various other things. However, the first release will have preset commands that you can enable and disable.

osu! Music Player is - as you can hopefully tell - a music player. It's visuals are inspiried by a rhythm game that goes by the name of osu!. The objective with this was to create a music player that has a low profile on screen, but still looks nice.
